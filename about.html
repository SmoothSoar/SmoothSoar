My story

From a passion for flight to a professional drone service.

I've always been fascinated by the world from above. What started as a hobby flying drones has evolved into a professional commitment to capturing stunning aerial imagery. I created Smooth Soar because I believe every project — whether it's a construction site, a dream home, a roof that needs inspecting, or a farm that needs monitoring — deserves to be seen from a new perspective.

I'm currently building this business from the ground up, and I'm committed to providing the highest quality service to every client. I'm FAA-certified, fully insured, and dedicated to delivering imagery that helps you make better decisions and showcase your work with pride.

My mission: To help you see your project from a new angle and capture imagery that tells your story.
